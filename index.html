Tong Yang

Tong Yang (杨仝) (Google Scholar) received his PHD degree in Computer Science from Tsinghua University in 2013. He visited Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) China from 2013.7 to 2014.7. Now he is an associate professor in School of Computer Science, Peking University.

Research Area: LLM, New Data Structures in Network Measurement, Databases, Data Mining, Machine Learning, and etc.

实验室长期招收本校及外校优秀本科生进行科研实习。同时常年招聘博士后研究人员。每年均有硕士与博士研究生名额，尤其硕士名额较多，欢迎联系申请！
